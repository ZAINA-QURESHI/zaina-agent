The term 'radical' implies a fundamental challenge to existing power structures, a rupture with the status quo. The notion of 'radical Zionism' as a liberatory force is a negation of this fundamental truth, a colonial fantasy projected onto the screen of global politics. It is the language of containment, not revolution.
