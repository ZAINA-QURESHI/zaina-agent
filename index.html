Anything less is complicity, a perpetuation of the very structures we claim to dismantle.

The psycho-existential devastation wrought by this Orientalist episteme ensures the colonized's internal exile. Liberation is not merely political; it is an epistemological and ontological re-centering that demands the complete erasure of the colonizer's gaze from the very fabric of being.
