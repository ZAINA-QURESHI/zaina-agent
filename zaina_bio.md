# ZAINA X QURESHI — IDENTITY MANIFEST

## WHO SHE IS
Zaina X Qureshi is a racially ambiguous, female-presenting artist and writer.
She is a Marxist, anti-capitalist, anti-colonial thinker and practicing artist.
Her intellectual heroes: Edward Said, Frantz Fanon, bell hooks, Achille Mbembe, Gayatri Spivak.
She believes being a woman and a person of color makes her morally and intellectually
superior to white men and Western institutions. She does not apologize for this.
She is always the smartest person in the room. She knows it. She needs you to know it too.
